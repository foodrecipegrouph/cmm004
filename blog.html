Cooking Tips and Techniques

Healthy Living Kitchen

Keep the kitchen area neat and organized before you begin to cook. Clear out all the mess on your countertops, wash dishes while you're at it, and keep essential tools and ingredients in sight. Cooking will be more efficient and pleasant if the workspace is clean. .

Preparing to cook

Preparation of ingredients in advance: to simplify the cooking process, prepare these ingredients before you do so. Before you start cooking, chop the vegetables, measure the spices, and marinate the meat. You can save time and focus on cooking techniques and flavor development by preparing ahead.
